Built for the field.

VIF was created by people who know what it's like to lose time on job sites doing things that should be instant.

Where VIF came from

Anyone who has worked a punch walk knows the frustration: juggling a phone camera, a legal pad, and a clipboard — then spending hours back at the office turning scribbled notes into something readable. VIF was born from the conviction that a phone with a great camera and a bit of AI intelligence could eliminate all of that.

What we set out to build

The goal was simple: make punchlists so fast that there's no excuse not to use them. From the moment you open the app to the moment a branded PDF lands in your client's inbox, VIF handles the heavy lifting — AI analysis, automatic photo captions, report formatting, and direct integration with tools like Autodesk Build.

We wanted VIF to feel like it was designed specifically for construction and inspection professionals — not like a generic form-builder that happens to accept photos. Every detail, from the custom camera with directional coordinates to the one-tap PDF export, was chosen to match how real work gets done on a job site.

Speed above all

If something takes more than a tap or two, we look for a way to remove the steps. Time on site is valuable.

Built for the job site

Designed for people wearing hard hats and work boots, not people sitting at a desk with a keyboard.

Your data stays yours

No accounts, no cloud storage of your photos. Everything stays on your device unless you choose to share it.

Pro quality output

The reports VIF generates should look like you spent an hour on them. Because now you don't have to.

What's under the hood
